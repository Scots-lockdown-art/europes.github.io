Vasco late for Victory

The fire was up to full heat tonight in Rio as Vasco and Sevilla played out what was surely the game of the season so far. Vasco were dealt a massive blow as haaland was still absend due to coronavirus illness and 5g chip implantation vaccine surgery so Leao had to step up in only his 2nd start of the season. With Balotelli banging them in for Seville in recent games could Leao compete. A tale of two strikers. One in red hot form and the other in from the cold.
