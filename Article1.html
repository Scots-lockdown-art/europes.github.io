Vascos late Voyage- 29/4/20


The fire was up to full heat tonight in Rio as Vasco and Seville played out what was surely the Super league game of the season so far. Vasco were dealt a massive blow before kick off as Haaland was still absent due to coronavirus and 5g chip implantation vaccine surgery so Leao had to step up in only his second start of the season. With Balotelli banging them in for Seville in recent games could Leao compete. A tale of two strikers. One in red hot form and the other in from the cold.


As expected when these two lock horns it was a night for attack as defenses were lost at sea. Leao dived right in with a quick goal to set the precedings. However, within minutes a fanatastic twenty yard strike from Ocampos silenced the home fans as it flew into top corner. No problem for Vasco though. The ship was steadied again as Leao preceded to fire in two more goals for a quick first half hat trick. The Samba beat was deafening, there was only one team here tonight and Sevilla were sinking deep.


How could Seville anchor their defense and prevent an onslaught. Seville manager Shaka had the answers! As he has often said "Best form of defense is attack." With players like De Bruyne and Balotelli one has to agree with these philosophies and so it was to be as out of nowhere they turned the game on its head. Some potent attacking from and poor Vasco defense got them back in it to get 3-3. Worse was to follow for the Vasos as despite looking like heading into half time all square a comedy of errors occured as Pepe Reina failed to collect the ball twice before it went through his legs leaving an easy tap in and a crazy turn around as Seville went from 3-1 down and out to leading at the break.


Could this game get any crazier? A Hamsik goal early into the second period levelled the tie once again. Another immediate reply straight from kick off as Sevilla regained there one goal lead and Vasco having to chase once more. Leao the first half hat trick hero became villain as he missed two easy chances. It looked as if the game was over. The Spanish invasion had conquered. With Vasos ship all but sunk heading to the 90th minute a patient attack and well played through pass from Griezmann sent Leao in to make ammends for recent misses and level the tie. A great escape but nothing less than the home team desereved and overall a thrilling end to the game. Or was it?


Deep into third minute of added time and Vasco happy to take the draw a steal of the ball and quick break. So much space, were Seville going for a late winner? It breaks to Greizmann again who slips in fresh super sub Castillejo who runs free in on goal for what will surely be last kick of the game. A forty yard run to the goalkeeper seemed to take about five minutes and ElPesidentes hands were surely shaking by this point, could he maintain his nerves and get a cruel late winner. Will he chip? will he finesse? will keeper come out? will keeper come out then go back?... then come out then go back again? all kind of thoughts running through his mind. But he had faith in his man Sami who made the decision for him with a well placed finesse into bottom corner. It was all over. 6-5 to Vasco. What a sensational game. Seville fans all at sea and drown in their misery.Vasco fans to drown on Corona beers throughout the night. One of the most historic games in Super league history! Elpesidente is apparently in ER with heart attack and Shaka_Zulu suicide watch. We hope to bring you good news about their fortunes soon.
